About

I lead the technical delivery of utility-scale battery storage projects across Europe — owning the full lifecycle from early-stage concept design and feasibility through to commissioning and long-term performance. As the primary technical point of contact for EPCs, suppliers, and clients, I prepare and review project drawings and specifications, run design reviews, and drive value engineering decisions that directly impact CapEx, efficiency, and availability.

Internally, I act as technical lead — guiding engineering teams, setting project standards, and ensuring designs meet compliance requirements before they reach site. On the regulatory side, I coordinate permitting documentation and liaise directly with authorities such as municipalities and grid operators (DSOs/TSOs) to keep projects moving through approval stages without delay.

With a product management background at Sungrow and an MSc in Sustainable Energy Systems, I bring both the commercial and technical lens needed to take BESS projects from proposal to operation.
